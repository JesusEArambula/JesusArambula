I was born in Ensenada, Baja California, Mexico, raised in a small neighborhood as a single child. After my 7th birthday my parents decided to move to San Diego with our new visa cards. I went to elementary school in Paradise Hills Elementary School where I attended 2nd and 3rd grade. My parents, once again wanted to move to another location, to Chula Vista in Sand Diego. I attended Rosebank Elementary School where I finished the rest of my grades. Shortly after, we moved back to Mexico. I attended middle school in various locations, including Tijuana, Rosarito, Ensenada, and San Quintin. Before even finishing middle school, my parents wanted to take one more trip to the United States, this time to San Francisco, where I attended and graduated from Raoul Wallenberg High School. I am currently attending City College of San Francisco majoring in Computer Science and planning to transfer to San Francisco State University.
